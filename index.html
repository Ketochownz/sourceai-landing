Where this started

Six years of running our own stores taught us what actually works.

Long before SourceAI existed, we were running ecommerce businesses — our own stores, our own customers, our own support queues. There weren't good tools for a lot of what we needed: customer service that didn't burn hours every day, systems that talked to each other, a way to keep up as the landscape kept shifting under us. So we learned to build it ourselves.

That's the honest origin of this company. We're not software theorists who picked "AI" as a market to chase — we're operators who hit the same walls every small business hits, built our way through them, and realised other businesses were stuck at the exact same walls.

Everything we build gets tested in our own businesses first. If it doesn't hold up running our stores or a real dealership floor, it doesn't go near a customer.

Lucid DMS

Proof of concept · Join the waitlist

An AI-native dealer management system for New Zealand automotive groups, currently in early development as a co-build with one design-partner dealership. Not a DMS replacement bolted onto old habits — an intelligence layer connecting Xero, CarJam and your listings, with an AI voice agent that answers the phone at 2am and a Kanban board that runs the workshop floor in real time. We're building this in partnership with forward-thinking dealer groups who want a say in how it's built.

01

Workshop Kanban

Runs the service department from booking to invoice on a real-time drag-and-drop board. Job cards can be created automatically by the AI voice agent — zero manual entry.

02

AI Voice Agent

Answers inbound service calls 24/7, confirms the vehicle by rego, and drops a populated job card on the board before the workshop opens.

03

Recon Pipeline

The moment a trade-in is appraised, Lucid auto-generates a full recon task list and cost budget with age/cost traffic-light colour coding.

04

Sales & Deal Jackets

New, used and demo stock through to delivery, with a live gross-profit calculator that updates instantly as finance, F&I and accessories are added.

05

Parts & Accessories

Imports and re-prices an entire OEM parts catalogue — 21,000+ line items — in under 60 seconds via chunked server-side import.

06

Group Dashboard

Live KPIs across every branch in the group on a single screen — something legacy per-branch systems were never built to do.

07

Grooming

Full-screen iPad PWA with damage-zone condition reports and a 10-point photo-verified quality sign-off before a car can be marked done.

08

Accounting & AP

Vehicle sales, recon costs and parts purchases push straight to the correct mapped Xero GL account — no manual bookkeeping, staged bills on partial delivery.

09

Intelligence

Ask "how many jobs are in progress right now" and get a live, accurate answer pulled straight from the database — your AI chief of staff.

10

Technician & Customer PWA

Mobile-installable apps: job timers and photo capture for technicians, live job-status tracking for customers, no phone tag either way.

11

Marketing

Type a plain-English segment — "WOF due in 30 days" — and Lucid builds the list and drafts the campaign copy automatically.

12

+ 8 more modules

QR vehicle hub, yard stocktake, service template library, DVI & approvals, admin controls, and a full customer CRM — all live today.

LucidOS

Flagship · Join the waitlist

The platform SourceAI is named after. A multi-tenant AI operating system — chat, CRM, voice and inventory intelligence — running as a fully isolated workspace for every business it serves. Built Shopify-first, now reaching into automotive as well.

01

AI chat widget

A ~15KB Shadow DOM widget embedded on the storefront — answers from the real catalogue and knowledge base, never invents products or prices.

02

CRM & lead capture

Every conversation becomes a lead with pipeline stage, source and activity timeline — synced to Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Omnisend and ActiveCampaign.

03

Chat-to-sale tracking

An HMAC-verified Shopify order webhook matches purchases back to the conversation that started them — real revenue attribution, not a vanity metric.

04

AI voice agent

A live voice receptionist for automotive dealers — structured booking flow, streaming transcript, deterministic extraction straight into the CRM.

05

Inventory intelligence

Claude-parsed supplier invoices, FEFO batch allocation and multi-currency landed cost — Lucid as the source of truth Shopify and Xero both read from.

06

AI Advisors

Marketing, HR & Legal, Operations and Health & Safety personas on tap, plus a general sandbox — configurable per workspace.

Why SourceAI

One engine. Three products. Built by someone who's worked the floor.

SourceAI is the studio behind three products. LucidOS is the flagship — the AI operating system the company is named after, running as an isolated workspace for 21 client businesses. Lucid DMS and CarTrader are separate, purpose-built systems for the dealership floor and the wholesale trade — each its own codebase, built by the same person.

Every product was built by someone who spent years inside a dealership before writing a line of code for one. That's not a marketing line — Lucid DMS and CarTrader were both built in the hours around six days a week on a dealership floor, which is why they solve the problems staff actually have instead of the ones a spreadsheet suggests.

The roadmap doesn't stop at automotive or ecommerce: LucidOS's chat, CRM and voice layer generalises to any SMB running on phone calls and spreadsheets instead of software — independent workshops, plumbing, electrical, building.
